Reserved seats

This option allows you to reserve a set number of seats for your meeting.

If you reserve seats for your meeting, it means that you will always have that number of spaces available for people to join in. Another meeting scheduled during the same time period can not take away any of your reserved seats.

You should enter a value and use the Check availability link to see if the number of seats you want to reserve is available for the duration of your meeting. If not, you will be told what the maximum number of seats available to you is.
